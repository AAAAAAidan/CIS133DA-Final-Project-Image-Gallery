About Image Gallery

Image Gallery is an image viewing site designed to let the user view free-use images while utilizing different features of HTML5 to teach me how to be a better programmer. All images were taken from pexels.com, a free image distributing site much like this one except that it's professionally made. You can find image maps on this page and under the contact page, and sources for images are below.
